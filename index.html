Hypothesis: Primates with a lower number of total grooming connections have connections with a higher frequency of grooming
Data: the data used is from https://networkrepository.com/mammalia-primate-association-10.php
It focuses on 'Primate Association Group 10' over a 3 month observation period.
The data makes no differentiation between giving and receiving, all grooming interaction is included and treated the same.
Frequency is not defined with units in the dataset.
For the purpose of these visuals, it is relative.
